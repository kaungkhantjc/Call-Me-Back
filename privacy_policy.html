Privacy Policies

Note that Call Me Back - CMB never collects any information from users. We don't even access Internet in Call Me Back - CMB app.

Indeed, it's absolutely an offline app.

What App collects

Call Me Back - CMB app uses READ_CONTACT permission to get user chosen phone number and store offline.

We dont access internet in this app and we never collect any information via online or offline from this app.

How App uses it

CMB uses user chosen phone number to store offline locally.

CMB sets the locally stored number to EditText after the app starts next time so that the user can send Call Me Back messages directly without choosing contact again.
